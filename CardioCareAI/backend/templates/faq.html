Frequently Asked Questions

Is CardioCareAI a replacement for a doctor?

No. Our tool provides risk predictions but should not be considered a substitute for medical consultation. Always consult a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis.

How accurate is the heart health prediction?

Our model has been trained on validated datasets and performs with high accuracy. However, no AI can guarantee 100% accuracy due to the variability of human health.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We prioritize user privacy and follow best practices to ensure that all submitted data is handled securely and confidentially.

Can I use this platform without a medical background?

Absolutely! CardioCareAI is designed to be user-friendly for both medical professionals and the general public.
